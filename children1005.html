 steps of Baddha Konasana – The Butterfly Pose

     5.Now breathe in (Inhale) and place a hand on both knees.
     6.Breathe out (exhale) and move your knees up and down till your knees touch the ground. (If you are not able to touch your knees to the ground during the process, in that case only move your knee up and down and avoid over exerting. Push down your knees as much you can).
