Steps of marjariasana cat pose
     1.Sit in Vajrasana, stand on the knees.
     2.Lean in the forward direction. Place the hands flat on the floor with palms down and fingers facing towards the forward direction.

     3.Keep the hands in a line with the knees. Keep the arms and thighs perpendicular to the floor. It is the starting position.

     4.Inhale a deep breath and raise the head along with putting stress on the spine in the downward direction, so that the back can turn into a concave shape.

     5.Expand the abdomen as much as possible without forcing; fill the lungs with maximum air possible. Hold breath for a minimum of 3 seconds. Exhale and lower the head while stretching the spine in the upward direction.

     6.Then contract the expanded abdomen and pull in the buttocks.

precutions of marjariasana cat pose
     1.One is advised to be careful while expanding and contracting the abdomen.

     2.Do not stretch the body beyond its limits as it may cause pain and strain muscles.

     3.While performing this asana during pregnancy one should stretch the abdomen only mildly.

     4.People having any kind of head or knee injury should avoid practicing this asana.
