 Benefits of Adho Mukhasvanasana – The Dog Pose
     1.Strengthens The Abdominal Muscles.
     2.The inversion of the downward facing dog pose is the boat pose.
     3.Improves Circulation.
     4.Improves Digestion.
     5.Tones The Hands And Feet.
     6.Decreases Anxiety.
     7.Works the back of your legs
     8.Improves blood flow to the brain
     9.On an emotional level downward facing dog helps turn everything on its head and helps us see things from a different angle.
     10.It helps boost self-confidence.
     11.Because of the increased blood flow to the top of the body, shoulder stand can help improve brain function and cognition and reduce anxiety and depression.
     12.Lengthens and straightens the spine, helping to relieve pain in the upper, middle and lower back.
     13.The body gets a 360-degree stretch in just one pose.
